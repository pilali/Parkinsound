Parkinsound Step Gate — modgui preview

In mod-ui, the side-view 1/4" jack plugs are drawn automatically by mod-ui via its built-in .mod-pedal-input-image / .mod-pedal-output-image classes and the {{#effect.ports.audio.input}} mustache loop. They're not shown here because this preview runs without mod-ui.
Click on a step sector (inner ring) to toggle on/off. Click on a tie segment (outer ring) to toggle the tie.
